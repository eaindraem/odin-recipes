Matcha

Description

This Iced Matcha Latte recipe is a creamy and refreshing beverage. Matcha Lattes can be made hot and cold! If you're missing your usual iced matcha latte from Starbucks or your neighborhood coffee shop, this recipe is for you! As a result, you'll be able to make your favorite drink at home!

In the afternoon, around 3pm, I get SUPER sleepy. I need a pick me up. And that pick me up during warmer weathered months is this: an Iced Matcha Latte. And in the afternoon when I need a bit of a pick-me-up and know I can't handle coffee or an espresso beverage (I'll be up all night), a matcha latte is the perfect go-to.

Ingredients

  • Matcha Powder
  • Oat Milk

Steps by Step Guide

  1. First, prepare your whisk by letting warm water run through it.
  2. Put some matcha powder in the matcha bowl.
  3. Pour some warm water into the bowl.
  4. Whisk the matcha and water with the whisk until there are enough bubbles.
  5. Transfer over to a cup and add ice and oat milk to taste.
